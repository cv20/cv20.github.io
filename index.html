Thai Tea

  • Time: 10 Minutes
  • Serves: 10 Cups

*Big Batch*
Quantity Ingredients
8 Cups Water
1 Cup Thai Tea Leaves
1 Cup Sugar
1 Pint Half-And-Half Milk


*Small Batch*
Quantity Ingredients
4 Cups Water
1/2 Cup Thai Tea Leaves
1/2 Cup Sugar
3 Tablespoons Half-And-Half Milk

Step 1: Boil water to medium-low.

Step 2: Add thai tea leaves.

Step 3: When you smell the aroma from the leaves, turn off fire immediately.

Step 4: Add in sugar and stir.

Step 5: Filter out leaves and let the tea cool down.

Step 6: Add in half-and-half milk.
